# Traditional Bank/Brokerage Flow

### Traditional Bank / Brokerage Flow (Capital One, Robinhood):

* **Accounts**: You deposit fiat, view balances.<br>
* **Actions**: Limited to sending money, paying bills, buying/selling pre-listed assets.<br>
* **Governance**: None. The platform makes all compliance, custody, and execution decisions.



* **Transparency**: Low. You trust statements and regulators.
